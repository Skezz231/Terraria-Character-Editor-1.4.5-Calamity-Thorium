# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

This project aims to maintain a respectful, practical and constructive environment for everyone.

Expected behavior:
- Be respectful and professional.
- Focus on technical discussion and project improvement.
- Accept feedback without hostility.
- Keep criticism specific and useful.
- Help create a welcoming space for contributors of all experience levels.

Unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment or intimidation
- Personal attacks
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Spam, trolling or deliberate disruption
- Publishing private information without permission

## Scope

This applies to issues, pull requests, discussions and other public interactions around the repository.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock or remove content that harms the project environment. Repeated or severe violations may lead to restrictions from further participation.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it privately through the contact method listed in `SUPPORT.md` or repository contact channels.
